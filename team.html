Key Personal Biographies

Our firm's passion and knowledge of the markets has been built over long and varied careers.

Scott Dobinson

Cheif Operating Officer

Scott Dobinson is our newly appointed Cheif Operating Officer at Ocean Captial. He is also a senior member of our firm's investment committee. Scott has joined Ocean Capital after a lengthy career at Croydon Council as their head of internal investments. Prior to this, he was head of Business Development at Trust Ford.

Nicholas East

Founder & Cheif Executive Officer

Nicholas East is our Founder & Cheif Executive Officer he is also a member of the frim's Investment Committee. Nicholas has over 12 years experience working with Tech start-ups across Europe and America. He spent most of his career working in the Goldman Sachs Investment group.

Alexander Rivers

Head of Business Development

Alexander Rivers heads up our Business Development team, His main responsibilities are qualifying new potential investment opportunities and working with clients to create a sustainable platform built around them. Alexander has a masters in Business Management from the University of Kent, he previously spent 10 years at Bloomberg.

George Bradley

Cheif Water Boy

George Bradley is one of a kind. No man is more crucial or dedicated to his job than George. He spends his day making sure the team is fully hydrated and nourished with snacks from our on-site kitchen. George also runs lunchtime PT & Yoga classes for all our employees to release built up energy and help promote a mind and body workplace environment.

Lily O'Donnell

Head of Human Resources

Lily O'Donnell spent most of her career as Director of Human Resources at the Crown Prosecution Service. She lead a team of 200 people and worked on some prolific cases over the years. After finally making the jump into the private sector, Lily now looks after the whole team at Ocean Capital and it has never ran smoother.

Ronnie Bushby

Facilities Manager

Ronnie Bushy looks after all of our offices frequently flying between the two. He uses his experience to help new clients that are looking for space. Ronnie has worked for some of the largest management companies in the UK. He's most recently been the building manager for the new Shard at London Bridge.
